Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We have a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with the original tags still attached. Please reach out to our customer service team to start a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries around the world. Shipping costs and delivery times depend on the location and will be calculated at checkout.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. We guarantee that all our products are 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized distributors and designers, ensuring premium quality.

How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you'll get a tracking number through email. You can also track your order status by logging into your customer account on our website.
